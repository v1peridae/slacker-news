It is often said that in Chinese, the word for “crisis” also means “opportunity”. This is nonsense. Crisis is 危机, which means “moment of danger”, and opportunity is 机会, which means “moment of potential”. They have 机 in common, but that would be like one Chinese person telling another, “Hey, did you know that in English, the word for ‘hand soap’ also means ‘hand gun’?”

Stardance will roughly double the number of Hack Clubbers. This isn’t a crisis. It is a challenge. But it is also an opportunity. This is our house and we’re about to have guests. So how are we getting ready?

**The Fire Department**

I want to set a clear expectation: from June 1 to September 15, moderation will be strict. Usually there is some slack in the system and we use that slack to do things like give people the benefit of the doubt, give people second chances, and let borderline cases slide. But [the point of moderation is to make the community a nice place](https://hackclub.slack.com/archives/C097BPLDYPQ/p1779306231003349?thread_ts=1778604181.477119&cid=C097BPLDYPQ). And from June 1 to September 15, there won’t be slack in the system.

While Stardance is running, please don’t try and find the exact line between acceptable and unacceptable behavior. Treat that line like a cliff: don’t stand on the edge. Instead of asking yourself “can I get away with this?” ask yourself, “how can I make our house a great place for newcomers?”

This is the big change, but we’ve made smaller ones too:
- New FD tools to help juggle more cases, which you can check out in <SlackChannel id="fire-dept-demo" />
- An automated slur detector
- A revised Code of Conduct
- A bot which automatically tells new users about Shroud and the CoC
- An internal dashboard to monitor the rate at which tickets are resolved

**The Fraud Squad**

- The Red Team program
- Improvements to the Fraud Squad’s internal dashboard so it shows true numbers instead of false ones
- New standard messages in fraud cases that offer users a path to prove they are innocent
- An experimental “validation ship” in which banned users can record themselves coding more and submit it for comparison to their historical activity

**The Special Activities Division**

- SAD will hold regular Show and Tell events for Stardance participants
- The calendar at events.hackclub.com will be integrated into the Stardance platform for all users

**The Nest Admins**

- Guides to IPv6 vs IPv4, PM2, and getting started on Nest
- Setting up a mediawiki
- Adding automated tools to detect network abuse
- Getting to >99% uptime

**You**

As the People Who Were Here First, you are about to be drafted as role models. Newcomers do not know what Hack Club is. They will look to you for answers. I’m challenging you to show up here with the conscious goal of being a really good person. Treat people better than you have to. Show people’s opinions more respect than you feel they deserve. Stay calm even in situations where you’re justified in getting angry.

Do you want Hack Club to be special? Do you want it to be life-changing? Do you want it to be the best community people have ever joined? This is your house; how do you want people to see it?

I said this is an opportunity. I mean it's an opportunity to change lives. I remember once, in #welcome-to-hack-club, someone said "I'm just processing that a place like this exists". You all have made something that doesn't and cannot exist anywhere else, and I'm so excited to watch you share the magic with new people. Thank you.
